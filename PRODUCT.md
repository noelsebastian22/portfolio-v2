# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, tech leads, recruiters, and business owners evaluating Noel Sebastian for senior frontend / Angular roles and freelance engagements. They arrive with a specific problem—scale, reliability, technical debt, or a missing specialist—and need to leave convinced he can own it.

## Product Purpose

A portfolio that converts credibility into conversations. It demonstrates deep Angular and frontend engineering expertise through real case studies, communicates availability, and makes booking a call the natural next step.

## Brand Personality

Bold, technical, warm.

Engineering craft with a human voice. Confident without being cold; precise without being sterile. The design should feel like the work itself: considered, performant, and slightly unexpected.

## Anti-references

- Corporate navy-and-gold SaaS minimalism.
- Generic template portfolios with identical icon + heading + text card grids.
- Loud startup illustration kits that decorate instead of communicate.
- Editorial-magazine affectation without engineering substance.

## Design Principles

1. **Show the craft, don't just claim it.** Every section should feel engineered, not described.
2. **One clear action per fold.** The path to "Book a Call" should never be ambiguous.
3. **Warmth through structure, not decoration.** Rhythm, spacing, and type carry the tone more than ornament.
4. **Distinctive over safe.** A portfolio that looks like everyone else's is a failed portfolio.
5. **Performance is part of the pitch.** Fast loads, smooth motion, and clean code demonstrate the work.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA minimum; aim for AAA where practical. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Maintain strong color contrast across the warm cream background. Keep skill-level indicators and data visualizations color-blind safe. Ensure all interactions are keyboard navigable.
